What Is This System?

In simple words: This is a mobile phone application (website that works like an app) that helps NEM Insurance sell damaged cars, electronics, and property faster and for more money.

When someone's car gets damaged in an accident and we pay their claim, we take the damaged car (called "salvage"). Right now, it takes 14+ days to sell this damaged car and we only get back 22 cents for every ₦1 worth of the car (very low!). Everything is done manually with phone calls, WhatsApp messages, and paperwork. We lose money because the process is slow and vendors don't trust the system.

This system puts everything online so vendors (people who buy damaged items) can see what's available on their mobile phones, bid on items from anywhere (like eBay), pay instantly with their bank cards, and complete everything in 3 days instead of 14 days.

🤖 AI & Machine Learning Capabilities

Intelligent Damage Assessment

AI analyzes photos of damaged assets in real-time and automatically determines:

  • Damage Severity: Categorizes as Minor (0-20%), Moderate (20-40%), Severe (40-60%), or Total Loss (60%+)
  • Estimated Salvage Value: Uses machine learning models trained on historical salvage sales data
  • Suggested Reserve Price: Recommends optimal starting bid (typically 70% of estimated value)
  • Risk Assessment: Flags items that may be difficult to sell

Benefits

  • No more guesswork - data-driven pricing decisions
  • Consistent valuation across all claims adjusters
  • Faster approvals - Salvage Manager gets AI recommendation instantly
  • Better recovery rates - optimal pricing attracts more bidders

When Claims Adjusters take photos at accident scenes, the system automatically:

📶 Offline-First Technology

Why This Matters in Nigeria

Network connectivity is unreliable at accident scenes (rural areas, underground parking, remote locations). Traditional apps would fail. Our system works regardless of internet availability.

How It Works

  • Progressive Web App (PWA): Works like a mobile app but runs in browser
  • Local Storage: Data saved on phone first
  • Smart Sync: Uploads automatically when connection returns
  • Image Compression: Reduces data usage by 80%
  • Queue Management: Pending actions saved and executed later

Real-World Scenario

Claims Adjuster at accident scene in remote area with poor network:

  1. Takes 8 photos of damaged vehicle
  2. Records voice note describing damage
  3. Fills case details in app
  4. Submits case - app saves locally
  5. Drives back to office with WiFi
  6. App auto-uploads everything
  7. Salvage Manager gets notification

Result: Zero downtime, zero data loss

3️⃣ Vendors - Registration System

Annual Vendor Fee

All vendors pay a yearly subscription fee to access the platform. Fee amount to be decided by System Administrators based on market dynamics and vendor feedback.

Tier 1: BVN Verified

Bid Limit: Up to ₦4,999,999

Registration Process:

  1. Fill basic form (Name, Email, Phone, DOB) OR sign up with Google/Facebook
  2. Verify phone via SMS code (OTP)
  3. Enter BVN (Bank Verification Number)
  4. System auto-verifies BVN matches name, phone, DOB
  5. If matched → Instantly approved!
  6. Gets "Verified BVN ✓" badge

Time: 3 minutes

Perfect for: Small traders, mechanics, informal sector

Tier 2: Full KYC

Bid Limit: ₦5,000,000 and above

Additional Requirements:

  • CAC certificate (business registration)
  • Tax ID (TIN)
  • Bank statement
  • Valid ID (NIN/Driver's license/Passport)

Verification:

  • System auto-verifies NIN, bank account, BVN match
  • Salvage Manager manually reviews documents
  • Gets "Verified Business ✓" badge

Time: 24 hours

Perfect for: Established businesses, large dealers

📱 Why Mobile-First? (Not Desktop)

The Nigerian Reality

70%+ of Nigerian internet users are mobile-only

Vendors are traders, mechanics, scrap dealers - they don't sit at desks

What "Mobile-First" Means

  • Progressive Web App (PWA): Works like mobile app but runs in browser (no app store download)
  • Offline Mode: Works without internet
  • Fast Loading: 2 seconds even on 3G
  • Touch-Optimized: Big buttons, easy to tap
  • Camera Integration: Take photos in browser
  • GPS Auto-Tag: Uses phone location
  • Push Notifications: Alerts when browser closed

Why Not Native App (Initially)?

  • PWA sufficient for MVP (Month 1-2)
  • Native apps in Phase 2 (Month 3-6) if needed
  • Saves development time and cost
  • No app store approval delays
  • Updates deploy instantly
  • Works on ALL phones (Android, iPhone, etc.)

Future: If demand is high, we'll build native iOS/Android apps in Phase 2

🔒 Security & Fraud Prevention

Pattern Detection

AI monitors:

  • Same person bidding against themselves (IP tracking)
  • New vendors with abnormal bid patterns
  • Multiple accounts using same BVN/phone
  • Payment defaults and late payers

Auto-Suspension

  • 3 confirmed fraud attempts → 30-day suspension
  • All bids canceled
  • Vendor notified immediately
  • Can appeal to System Admin

Data Protection (NDPR Compliant)

  • BVN Encryption: Only last 4 digits shown (****7890)
  • Password Security: Military-grade encryption (bcrypt)
  • Data in Transit: TLS 1.3 encryption
  • Activity Logs: Cannot be deleted, stored 2 years
  • User Rights: Can export/delete their data
  • Consent: Required before data collection

Security Standards

  • OWASP Top 10 protection
  • Penetration testing (quarterly)
  • External security audit (before launch)
  • 99.5% uptime SLA

Solution: BVN API verification, fraud detection, Tier 2 manual review, auto-suspension

Risk: Payment defaults

Solution: Escrow wallet, 24hr deadline, vendor suspension, reputation system

Risk: Low vendor adoption

Solution: Simple Tier 1 registration, SMS marketing, referral bonuses, gamification

Risk: Informal traders resistance

Solution: WhatsApp onboarding videos, in-person training, hybrid model
