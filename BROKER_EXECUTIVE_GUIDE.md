# Identity Collection System - Executive Guide

## Overview

The Identity Collection System helps insurance brokers collect and verify National Identity Numbers (NIN) for individual clients and Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) registration details for corporate clients, ensuring compliance with NAICOM and NIIRA regulatory requirements.

## Quick Start Guide

### 1. Login & Access
- Access the system through your broker dashboard
- Navigate to "Identity Collection" section
- All identity collection activities are centralized in one location

### 2. Upload Customer Lists

**Template Download:**
- Download the appropriate template (Individual or Corporate)
- Templates ensure all required fields are included
- Individual template: Name, Email, Phone, Policy Number
- Corporate template: Company Name, Email, Registration Number, Policy Number

**File Upload:**
- Upload completed CSV or Excel files (max 10MB)
- System validates file format and required columns
- Preview shows first 10 rows for verification
- Auto-detects email and name columns

**Data Review & Editing:**
- Click on any cell in the preview table to edit customer data
- Make corrections to names, emails, phone numbers, or other fields
- Click the checkmark icon at the end of each row to save changes
- System validates edits and highlights any errors
- All edits are preserved when creating the list

**List Creation:**
- Give your list a descriptive name
- Select the email column for sending verification links
- System creates the list with your edited data and shows progress dashboard

### 3. Send Verification Links

**Individual Clients (NIN Verification):**
- Select entries from your customer list
- Click "Request NIN" to send verification links
- Links expire in 7 days for security
- Customers receive email with secure verification page

**Corporate Clients (CAC Verification):**
- Select corporate entries from your list
- Click "Request CAC" to send verification links
- Corporate clients must upload three CAC documents:
  - Certificate of Incorporation
  - Particulars of Directors
  - Share Allotment (Status Update)

### 4. Monitor Progress

**Dashboard Overview:**
- View total lists, entries, and verification progress
- Real-time status updates as customers complete verification
- Filter by Individual or Corporate lists
- Export completed verifications

**List Details:**
- Click any list to view detailed progress
- See individual customer status (Pending, Link Sent, Verified, Failed)
- Resend links to customers who haven't responded
- View verification details and uploaded documents

### 5. Handle Verification Results

**Successful Verifications:**
- System automatically validates NIN/CAC against provided information
- Verified customers show green "Verified" status
- Download verification reports for compliance records

**Failed Verifications:**
- Red "Failed" status indicates verification issues
- Click status to see specific failure reasons
- Customers can retry verification if needed
- Contact customers directly for data corrections

### 6. CAC Document Management

**For Corporate Clients:**
- View uploaded CAC documents directly in the system
- Documents are encrypted and securely stored
- Preview documents without downloading
- Download documents when needed for compliance

**Document Status Indicators:**
- Green checkmark: Document uploaded and verified
- Red X: Document missing or failed validation
- Click indicators to preview documents

### 7. Compliance & Reporting

**Regulatory Compliance:**
- System ensures NAICOM/NIIRA compliance automatically
- All verification attempts are logged for audit
- Encrypted storage meets data protection requirements

**Export & Reporting:**
- Export verified customer data to CSV/Excel
- Generate compliance reports for regulators
- Activity logs track all system actions
- Audit trail for regulatory inspections
